THE LAST MONSOON

A SHORT FILM • 2023

Short Film • 2023

A poignant tale of a fisherman's struggle against climate change and the changing monsoon patterns that threaten his livelihood. This 15-minute short film explores themes of environmental consciousness, family bonds, and adaptation in the face of nature's unpredictability.
