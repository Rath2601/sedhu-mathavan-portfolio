THE SILENT PROTEST

A SHORT FILM • 2022

Short Film • 2022

A powerful social commentary about a mute protester who finds his voice through art and silent resistance. This 20-minute experimental film explores themes of communication, social justice, and the power of non-verbal expression in a world dominated by noise.
