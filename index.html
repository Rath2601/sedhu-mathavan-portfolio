About

The Filmmaker's Journey

My passion for storytelling began in the theater, where I discovered the power of narrative and character development. Transitioning to film, I've worn multiple hats - from actor to assistant director to screenplay writer. Each role has deepened my understanding of the cinematic craft and strengthened my vision for bringing stories to life on screen.

Having worked on over 15 short films, I've learned that great cinema emerges from the collaboration between compelling scripts and visionary direction. My screenplays have been recognized at festivals across India, and I'm now ready to step behind the camera to direct my own stories.
