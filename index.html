ABOUT

THE JOURNEY OF A STORYTELLER

My Journey

I'm Sedhu Mathavan R S, a curious yet calm and composed individual from Pattukkottai, Tamil Nadu. I graduated in Civil Engineering from Alagappa Government Engineering College, Karaikudi, and worked as a Software Engineer at TCS for two years.

Somewhere, life got stuck between IF conditions and FOR loops, and I found my way back to what I always wanted to do — CINEMA. I have always dreamed of becoming a filmmaker since my school days, but like many from a middle-class Indian family, I didn't have the chance to think about it earlier.

So, I finally decided to type 'BREAK' from everything and follow my calling. I'm now pursuing Screenplay Writing at SRFTI, Kolkata, exploring the craft of filmmaking and discovering how to shape emotions, experiences, and ideas into stories that truly resonate on screen.
