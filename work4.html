MONSOON DREAMS

A SHORT FILM • 2022

Short Film • 2022

A coming-of-age story about a young girl's dreams and aspirations during the monsoon season in rural India. This 16-minute poetic film captures the beauty of childhood innocence, family bonds, and the transformative power of nature through stunning cinematography and heartfelt storytelling.
